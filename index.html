About Me

I am a driven and passionate recent high school graduate with a strong foundation in programming and a keen interest in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, particularly in the areas of computer vision and natural language processing. Currently seeking admission to a bachelor's program, I am eager to deepen my technical expertise in AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science.

Throughout my academic journey and extracurricular activities, I have developed strong problem-solving skills and a proactive approach to learning. I have hands-on experience building projects, including a Calculator App, Flappy Bird Game, YouTube Video Downloader, URL Shortener, Currency Converter, and a Modern Todo List App. My technical skills include Python (Intermediate), HTML/CSS (Beginner), and familiarity with Microsoft Office tools and basic Figma. I am actively expanding my knowledge through certifications in Python for Data Science, HTML & CSS, Data Analytics, Excel & PowerPoint, and UI & UX Designing.

Beyond technology, I am committed to environmental advocacy, having volunteered with Tarumitra (UN ECOSOC Accredited) for several years, where I guided junior volunteers and participated in numerous environmental camps. I have also demonstrated leadership and teamwork through various competitions, including earning 2nd Position (School) and 61st Rank (State) in the International Mathematics Olympiad, and completing a National Adventure Program.

With proficiency in English and Hindi, and basic knowledge of German and Spanish, I am well-positioned for global opportunities. I am enthusiastic about leveraging technology to create innovative solutions and am looking for opportunities to contribute my skills and continue learning in a dynamic environment.
